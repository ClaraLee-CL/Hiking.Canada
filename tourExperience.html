Winter Experience: Rockies Winter Mini Tour

perfect for beginners

A private Kananaskis mini tour starts with your Canadian Rockies Experience arriving at your accommodations with fresh pastries and hot beverages in hand. This tour is the perfect opportunity to experience a lesser known area in the Canadian Rockies. The drive is jaw dropping in itself with many opportunities for scenic photographs. An easy walk allows an immersion into nature in a relaxed environment. The journey continues with a stop at a mountain lodge for lunch. Soak up the scenery that this special location has to offer.

Summer LAKES, TRAINS & CANYON EXPERIENCE

A Private Hiking Tour

The morning begins with locally baked pastries and hot beverages from your guide, while you are transported to the trail head in a luxury SUV. An immersive full day Kananaskis experience, this tour is highlighted by a hike along and above the iconic Kananaskis Lakes. The forested trail leads to a mountain amphitheatre providing a breathtaking back drop to Rawson Lake. Lunch is served while overlooking Kananaskis Lakes to relax and reflect on the experience. The return journey through the scenic Spray Valley is stunning with ample opportunities for photographs that capture the magic of this renowned valley. A picnic stop on the way home ensures your day finishes with spectacular memories.
